    *P3 (EU) or P100 (US) face mask (follow instructions carefully how to wear so no leak). N95 (US standard) also good but not quite as good as P100.

    *Sealed eye protection, or wrap around goggles if sealed unavailable.

    *Start to avoid large crowds and start to minimise social contact with others.

    *No kissing/hugging friends.

    *No shaking hands.

    *Wash your hands frequently and thoroughly throughout the day. Use warm soapy water, wash all hand surfaces - palm and top of hand, between fingers and thumbs, circle tips of fingers and thumb on soapy palm to wash under finger nails. Use running water to rinse thoroughly, using the same actions you used to wash.

    *Do not share hand towels in work places or at home.

    *Do not touch your eyes nose or mouth throughout the day.

    *In workplaces frequently wipe all communal surfaces, door handles and buttons with 70% alcohol wipes.

    *At work and at home properly close toilet lids when flushing.

    *Use a clean tissue to touch handles when opening and closing doors or touching elevator buttons in workplaces, or other places where people mix. Afterwards bin the tissue.

    *Before you enter your home leave shoes, coat and bags either outside if possible or inside in closed bags very near the front door. If coronavirus known to be in your area also remove top clothes layer and change into new.

    *Then also before enter house wipe hands with a 70% alcohol wipe after have finished shoe removal etc, then wipe all mobile devices carefully with a 70% alcohol wipe.

    *The shoe removal, hand wiping, phone wiping etc should lower the amount of virus getting into your home.

    *When get in house do proper soap hand wash straight away.
