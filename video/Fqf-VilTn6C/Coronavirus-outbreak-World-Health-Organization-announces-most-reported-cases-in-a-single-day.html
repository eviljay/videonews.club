Coronavirus outbreak: World Health Organization announces most reported cases in a single day

The World Health Organization's (WHO) director general Tedros Adhanom said on Wednesday that they had seen the most reported cases of the virus in a single day since the novel coronavirus outbreak started, adding that in total there were 24,363 confirmed cases of the illness in China.

He also said on Wednesday that with 99 per cent of novel coronavirus cases confined to China, it presented in their opinion a "window of opportunity" to prevent the virus from further spreading worldwide.
