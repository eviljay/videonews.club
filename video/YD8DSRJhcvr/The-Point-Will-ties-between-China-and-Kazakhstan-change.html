The Point: Will ties between China and Kazakhstan change?

Kazakhstan's President Nursultan Nazarbayev announced his resignation in a nationally televised address on Tuesday. Why did Nazarbayev, having led the country since the collapse of the former Soviet Union in the early 1990s, step down now? How can power be transferred peacefully to the new generation of leaders? And will ties between China and Kazakhstan change? #Kazakhstan

Panelists: Su Hao, professor and director of the Center for Strategic and Peace Studies, China Foreign Affairs University; Bayan Konirbayev, a Kazakh national based in Beijing who specializes in the Belt and Road Initiative.
